Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for both Farmers and Buyers

👩‍🌾 How do I join as a farmer?

Create an account, select the "Farmer" role, and start listing your produce.

🛒 How can buyers place orders?

Buyers can browse available produce, contact the farmer, and arrange delivery or pickup directly.

💰 Are there listing fees?

No, FarmConnect is free to use with no platform charges for now.

🔐 Is FarmConnect secure?

Yes! We use secure login and encrypted data to protect all users.
