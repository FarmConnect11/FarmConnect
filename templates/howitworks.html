How FarmConnect Works

1. Farmers List Produce

Farmers register and upload details of their fresh produce with quantity, price, and images.

2. Customers Browse Products

Customers explore produce by category, compare prices, and view detailed product and farmer info.

3. Chat for Negotiation

Buyers and farmers can chat directly to negotiate pricing or clarify details before purchase.

4. Place Order

Customers place an order and agree on pickup or delivery options with the farmer.

5. Direct Transaction

Farmers and customers complete the deal directly, reducing middlemen and costs.
